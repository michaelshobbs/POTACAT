# ECHOCAT always-on fix, idle WSPR/SSTV, and PSKReporter

**Headline:** v1.8.16 fixes an upgrade snag where **ECHOCAT wouldn't start** for some users, lets you choose **WSPR or SSTV** for passive receive while the station is idle, and adds **PSKReporter** reporting for your FT8/FT4 decodes — plus WSPR polish and a Flex reconnect fix.

## 🛠️ ECHOCAT starts again after upgrading

A few operators upgraded and found **ECHOCAT wouldn't run** — "Pair my device" said the server was off, but there was no longer a switch to turn it on (ECHOCAT went always-on a while back). The cause was a per-profile flag that some upgraded/multi-op profiles came up without, so the always-on server never started in the GUI. POTACAT now guarantees the ECHOCAT server starts regardless of profile state, and the pairing screen's "enable it in Settings" hints were corrected (there's no toggle — just relaunch). — thanks **KE6ZC** for the report

## 🌙 WSPR or SSTV while idle

The old "Auto-SSTV on idle" is now **"WSPR or SSTV While Idle."** When the station sits idle for your timeout, POTACAT can open **either** mode to passively receive — pick from a dropdown in Settings → Station (and on the first-run welcome screen):

- **SSTV** (unchanged): flips to 20 m (14.230) in the daytime and 40 m (7.171) at night, based on your grid.
- **WSPR**: choose **band hopping** to sweep the WSPR bands, or pin a **single band**.

## 📡 PSKReporter reporting

POTACAT can now report the FT8/FT4 stations JTCAT decodes to **[PSKReporter](https://pskreporter.info)** (the standard IPFIX/UDP feed), so your receptions show up on the PSKReporter map and propagation tools. It's a simple on/off in Settings — no `settings.json` editing required.

## 〰️ WSPR polish

- **Country and Region columns** in the WSPR spot list, and **callsigns on the WSPR map are now QRZ-clickable**.
- **Tuning to WSPR no longer stops the JTCAT engine** — switching to WSPR while nothing is decoding keeps the engine alive instead of dropping it.

## 🐞 Bug fixes

- **FlexRadio: silent / un-tunable after a reconnect** — on reconnect POTACAT could bind to its *own* stale GUI client session, leaving you with no audio and no tuning. It no longer does.
- **ECHOCAT pairing panel showed a stale "free trial"** instead of your actual Cloud subscription card.
